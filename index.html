About Us

Welcome to Bridges of Love, a nurturing haven for children in need. Our home is a place where love, care, and support come together to create a safe and joyful environment for every child who walks through our doors.

Our Beginning

This orphanage, called "Love Center for the Children" is outside Kisii, Kenya, was started in 2012.

Our Mission

we are dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children. Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and loving environment where these children can grow, thrive, and realize their full potential.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where every child is loved, nurtured, and given the opportunity to thrive regardless of their circumstances. We envision a society that recognizes and values the inherent worth and potential of every orphaned or vulnerable child.

Our Objectives

Provide Safe and Nurturing Care: Our primary objective is to ensure the safety and well-being of every child under our care. We strive to create a loving and nurturing environment that meets their physical, emotional, and psychological needs.

Our Plans

At Bridges of Love, we have a clear vision and strategic plans in place to ensure that every child receives the best possible care and support
